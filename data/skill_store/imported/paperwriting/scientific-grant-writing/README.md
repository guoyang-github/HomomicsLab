# Research Grants Skill

## Overview

Comprehensive skill for writing competitive research grant proposals for both **U.S. federal agencies** and **Chinese national funding agencies**.

### U.S. Agencies
- **NSF** (National Science Foundation)
- **NIH** (National Institutes of Health)
- **DOE** (Department of Energy)
- **DARPA** (Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency)

### Chinese Agencies
- **NSFC** (National Natural Science Foundation of China / 国家自然科学基金委)
- **NKRD** (National Key R&D Program / 国家重点研发计划)
- **Postdoc Funds** (China Postdoctoral Science Foundation / 中国博士后科学基金)
- **Provincial Funds** (各省自然科学基金)

## What This Skill Provides

### Agency-Specific Guidance

Detailed reference materials for each funding agency including:
- Mission and priorities
- Review criteria and scoring
- Proposal structure and page limits
- Budget requirements
- Submission processes
- Tips for competitive applications

### Core Components

- **Specific Aims Pages** (NIH): Template and detailed guide for the critical 1-page aims page
- **Project Summaries** (NSF): Template for the required Overview, Intellectual Merit, and Broader Impacts
- **Broader Impacts**: Comprehensive strategies for NSF's equally-weighted review criterion
- **Budget Justification**: Templates and examples for personnel, equipment, travel, and supplies
- **Review Criteria**: Understanding what reviewers look for at each agency
- **立项依据** (NSFC): Guidance for literature review and research basis sections
- **创新点** (NSFC): Strategies for articulating innovation in Chinese proposals
- **年度研究计划** (NSFC): Year-by-year planning templates
- **考核指标** (NKRD): Quantifiable assessment metrics for national key projects

## Key Features

### NSF Proposals

- **Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts** (equally weighted)
- Strategies for substantive, measurable broader impacts
- Integration of research and education
- Broadening participation in STEM
- 15-page project description limits (most programs)

### NIH Proposals

- **Specific Aims Page**: The most critical page (detailed 1-page guide included)
- **Research Strategy**: Significance, Innovation, Approach sections
- **Preliminary Data**: Essential for R01 applications
- Rigor and reproducibility requirements
- Modular vs. detailed budgets
- Resubmission strategies (A1 applications)

### DOE Proposals

- **Energy relevance** and alignment with DOE mission
- **Technology readiness levels** (TRLs)
- National laboratory collaborations
- Cost sharing requirements (especially ARPA-E)
- Commercialization pathways
- User facilities access

### DARPA Proposals

- **DARPA-hard problems**: High-risk, high-reward
- **Heilmeier Catechism**: The 8 critical questions
- Program Manager engagement (critical!)
- Phase-based structure with milestones
- Technology transition planning
- Demonstration and prototypes

### NSFC Proposals (国家自然科学基金)

- **面上项目 (General Program)**: ~50万元/4年，自主选题基础研究
- **青年科学基金 (Youth Program)**: ~30万元/3年，<35岁（男）/<40岁（女）
- **重点项目 (Key Program)**: ~200-300万元/5年，深入系统研究
- **优青/杰青**: 人才项目，强调学术贡献和潜力
- **评审标准**: 创新性 + 科学价值 + 研究方案 + 研究基础
- **400字摘要**（严格限制），需中英文双语
- **研究基础**独立章节，占较大比重
- **定额补助**（包干制），无需详细预算论证

### 国家重点研发计划 (NKRD)

- **指南导向**: 所有申报必须严格对应发布的指南方向
- **项目-课题两级管理**: 项目下设若干课题
- **青年科学家项目**: ~300-500万元/3年，<40岁
- **里程碑考核**: 明确的、量化的考核指标
- **详细预算**: 按科目编制，需详细测算说明
- **通常需要现场答辩**
- **弱化论文要求**，强调目标完成情况

### 博士后/省级基金

- **博士后科学基金**: 5-18万元，进站18个月内申请
- **省级基金**: 5-100万元，强调服务地方发展
- **可作为申请国家基金的"练兵场"**

## Best Practices

### Start Early
- NSF/NIH proposals: Start 3-6 months before deadline
- DOE/DARPA proposals: 4-6 months (especially if involving national labs)
- NSFC proposals: Start 3-6 months before March deadline
- NKRD proposals: Start 4-6 months before guide release

### Get Feedback
- Mock review sessions
- Colleagues in and outside your field
- Institutional grant support offices
- Program officers (when appropriate)
- **NSFC**: 请有获批经验的同事或导师预审
- **NKRD**: 参加单位组织的预评审和模拟答辩

### Understand Review Criteria
- NSF: Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts (equal weight)
- NIH: Significance, Investigator, Innovation, Approach, Environment (scored 1-9)
- DOE: Technical merit, qualifications, budget, relevance
- DARPA: Innovation, impact, team, feasibility, transition
- **NSFC**: 创新性(30%) + 科学价值(30%) + 研究方案(25%) + 研究基础(15%)
- **NKRD**: 符合度(20%) + 方案先进性(25%) + 考核指标(20%) + 团队(20%) + 预算(15%)

### Common Success Factors

✅ Clear, compelling significance and innovation
✅ Strong preliminary data (NIH, DOE, NSFC)
✅ Detailed, rigorous methodology
✅ Realistic timeline and budget
✅ Specific, measurable outcomes
✅ Strong team with relevant expertise
✅ Integration of broader impacts (NSF)
✅ Technology transition plan (DOE, DARPA)
✅ **NSFC-specific**: 紧密围绕关键科学问题，创新点有具体支撑
✅ **NKRD-specific**: 考核指标具体可量化，紧密对应指南方向
✅ **NSFC-specific**: 研究基础与申请内容高度相关

### Tips for Chinese Proposals

**For NSFC Proposals**:
1. Start with 立项依据 - ensure comprehensive literature review with critical analysis
2. Perfect your 400-character abstract - it is read by all reviewers
3. Develop 2-4 concrete innovation points with specific supporting evidence
4. Ensure 研究基础 directly supports proposed research
5. Use figures (技术路线图) to improve readability
6. Write 年度研究计划 with specific, achievable yearly goals
7. Be cautious with words like "首次" (first time) - only use with evidence
8. Get feedback from colleagues who have successfully obtained NSFC funding

**For NKRD Proposals**:
1. Strictly align with the guide direction - every element should correspond
2. Set concrete, quantifiable assessment metrics (考核指标)
3. Prepare thoroughly for the defense/presentation (答辩)
4. Ensure clear collaboration mechanisms for multi-institution projects
5. Budget should align with milestones and deliverables

**For Provincial Funds**:
1. Emphasize regional relevance and contribution to local development
2. Can use as stepping stone to national-level funding
3. Generally shorter preparation time - watch for announcements

## Tips for Maximum Effectiveness

### For NSF Proposals
1. Start with Specific Aims/Objectives (even though not required)
2. Develop broader impacts with same rigor as research plan
3. Use figures and diagrams liberally (make it skimmable)
4. Address both review criteria explicitly
5. Get feedback from outside your immediate field

### For NIH Proposals
1. Perfect your Specific Aims page first (10+ drafts)
2. Include substantial preliminary data
3. Address rigor and reproducibility explicitly
4. Identify potential problems proactively with alternatives
5. Make sure your aims are independent but synergistic

### For DOE Proposals
1. Emphasize energy relevance and impact
2. Include quantitative metrics (cost, efficiency, emissions)
3. Develop pathway to deployment or commercialization
4. Consider national laboratory partnerships
5. Address technology readiness levels

### For DARPA Proposals
1. Contact the Program Manager early (essential!)
2. Attend Proposers Day events
3. Focus on breakthrough innovation (10x, not 10%)
4. Answer the Heilmeier Catechism explicitly
5. Develop clear transition strategy

### For NSFC Proposals
1. Start with 立项依据 - comprehensive literature review is the foundation
2. Perfect your 400-character abstract (10+ drafts recommended)
3. Ensure 创新点 are concrete and supported by evidence
4. Develop 年度研究计划 with specific, measurable yearly goals
5. Ensure 研究基础 directly and strongly supports proposed work
6. Choose 申请代码 carefully - it determines reviewer assignment
7. Use 技术路线图 (technical roadmap figures) liberally
8. Get feedback from successful NSFC awardees in your field

### For NKRD Proposals
1. Strictly study and align with the guide direction
2. Set concrete, quantifiable 考核指标 from day one
3. Prepare thoroughly for 答辩 (defense presentation)
4. Develop clear collaboration mechanisms for multi-institution projects
5. Ensure budget aligns with milestones and deliverables

### For Provincial Funds
1. Emphasize regional relevance and contribution to local development
2. Use as stepping stone to national-level funding
3. Watch for shorter announcement-to-deadline windows
