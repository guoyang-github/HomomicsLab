## Tips for Strong NSF Biosketches

1. **Relevance First**: The 5 most-related products should directly support your proposed work
2. **Show Breadth**: The 5 other significant products show broader impact
3. **Synergistic Activities Matter**: NSF cares deeply about broader impacts - be specific
4. **Keep Current**: Update before each submission
5. **Be Honest**: Only list products where you made a substantive contribution
6. **Format Consistently**: Use consistent citation format throughout
