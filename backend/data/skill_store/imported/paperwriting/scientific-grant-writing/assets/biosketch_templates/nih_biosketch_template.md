## Tips for Strong Biosketches

1. **Tailor the Personal Statement**: Customize for each proposal
2. **Choose Contributions Strategically**: Select those most relevant to proposed work
3. **Quantify Impact**: Include citation counts, h-index, or other metrics if strong
4. **Show Productivity**: Demonstrate consistent publication record
5. **Address Gaps**: If you have career interruptions, address them in the personal statement
6. **Update Regularly**: Keep publications and grants current
